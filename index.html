Impetus

In a world of growing uncertainty and mistrust, it is more important than ever to seek practical solutions with trustless truth via distributed ledger technologies.

Mission

To support inquisitive and innovative minds in directing creativity and passion for building a better world through collaboration and co-operation via the power of trustless truth.

Who Can Join?

High schoolers, college & university students, and new grads.

What To Submit?

A deck and a paper. Video optional. Finalists need a poster.

International Blockchain Olympiad (IBCOL)

IBCOL is an annual web3 solution design competition for students and fresh graduates from any field of study or with plans to work in any industry. IBCOL hosts a global community of young people aspiring to find their place in the 21st century digital economy via the realm of trust technologies.
