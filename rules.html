IBCOL 2024 Program

Building a better world, one block at a time

Registration

IBCOL 2024 offers 2 programs: 

  • Contestants: compete in the 7th International Blockchain Olympiad and participate in special tour of academic, industry, and government facilities related to blockchain, AI, and other emerging technologies around The Netherlands.
  • Observers: join the [aforementioned] special tour

Eligible participants must be either current or recent students from: 

  • Secondary-level academic institution (e.g. middle school, high school, collegiate institute, gymnasium, lyceum, etc)
  • Tertiary-level academic institution (e.g. college, university, Hochschule)

Contestant Teams have:

  • Minimum 1 and maximum 6 eligible individuals
  • May not be more than 50% identical to another team

Observers must be eligible individuals.

A contestant may represent a country of their choice by  : 

  • Birthright
  • Citizenship
  • Permanent Residency
  • Academic Affiliation
  • Academic Nomination

If a contestant is stateless, the Competitions Committee (IBCOL-24CC") may assign a country for the contestant to represent.

Contestant Teams must register before submitting materials. Unless granted special exemption due to force majeure, Contestant Teams and Observer Delegations must register by Monday 30 September 2024

Project Deliverables

Competition Categories

Teams pick UN SDG to fall under one of 3 categories:

  • Social Impact
  • Economic Impact
  • Environmental Impact

Guides

Teams may reference these concepts for project development:

  • Problem Statement
  • States-of-the-Art Overview
  • Solution Overview
  • Solution Design
  • Challenges

Material Submissions

Teams portray their projects with:

  • A Proposal Slide Deck
  • A Whitepaper (In IEEE Conference Format)
  • An Video File (Optional)
  • A Poster (Finalist Only)

Submissions Deadline

Teams submit their materials differently, if they represent countries:

  • With accredited organizers: refer to the schedule and deadlines set by the local representative(s) and/or organizing committee
  • Without accredited organizers: the deadline for submitting has been extended to Monday 30 September 2024 — if teams require an extension due to force majeure, contact us to present your case and make request

Evaluation

Criteria

Teams shall be evaluated by a common foundation framework :

Commercial Depth40%
Technical Depth30%
Presentability20%
Originality10%

Process

Contestants will be evaluated by Adjudicators
Candidates will be invited to IBCOL 2024
Finalists who accept the invitation will travel to The Netherlands for the IBCOL 2024 Finals

Finals

Hosted in Amsterdam

Travel to Europe

If you require a Schengen Visa, you may consider to register as an Observer and request the supporting documentation that may assist with the application process.

Exclusive Tours

Exciting and exclusive activities are prepared for before the IBCOL 2024 Finals to enrich the experience of participants and prepare them for a higher level of competition at the main event.

Meet the Best & Brightest

The IBCOL 2024 Finals are held on Thursday 21 November and Friday 22 November at the campus of Hogeschool InHolland in the Sluisbuurt district of Amsterdam.

    International Blockchain Olympiad (IBCOL)

    IBCOL is an annual web3 solution design competition for students and fresh graduates from any field of study or with plans to work in any industry. IBCOL hosts a global community of young people aspiring to find their place in the 21st century digital economy via the realm of trust technologies.
